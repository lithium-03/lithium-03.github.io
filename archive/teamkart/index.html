Cars
We have completed the design and manufacture of 3 cars over the past few years. Our first car, the KX-1, was completed in 18 months. We participated in Formula Student 2010 thus making us the only team from Eastern India and amongst the few Indian teams to be a part of this prestigious event. We stood 9th in the cost report analysis, making us one of the most cost-efficient teams We were amongst the 6 shortlisted teams for the Airbus Team Work Award Having completed our second car, the K-1, in just 7 months, we participated in Formula Student 2011. This being the first time we actually took the car to the event, we managed to outperform the other rookie teams. We are amongst the top 3 rookie teams at the event Just like the previous year, we were once again nominated for the Airbus Team Work Award We started designing our our third car, the K-2, in the December of 2011. The manufacture of the car took us about 5 months and we began its testing in the June of 2012. We participated in Formula Student 2013 where we stood 67th overall. In 2015 with the now upgraded and rechristened K-2.2, we participated in Formula Design Challenge 2015 held at Coimbatore, where we brought home silverware in Cost Report and Business presentation events.
The Team
Formula SAE at IIT Kharagpur took a solid form in October 2008, when a group of enthusiastic students got together to form TeamKART. It was started up as a project under the aegis of the Sponsored Research and Industrial Consultancy Cell (SRIC) of IIT Kharagpur and under the purview of the Mechanical Department. Since then there has been a steady rise in our workforce and our potential as a team. Our journey began with our first car, the KX-1. We faced many problems in our fledgling years, mainly due to a lack of financial aid and technical knowledge. However with the help of some determination and a lot of perseverance, we rose above all obstacles and managed to build our car. Our first successful run gave a huge boost to our confidence and also served the purpose of showing the Institute, our sponsors and our alumni, our ambition and determination to succeed. After that we, in the span of less than 2 years, achieved the feat of competing in an international event with our second car, the K-1. We are guided by our extremely competent faculty from the Department of Mechanical Engineering. Our faculty advisors are: Prof. Amiya R. Mohanty Prof. Kingshook Bhattacharyya Prof C.S. Our Partners
TeamKART is always on the lookout for new partners who can help the project. With the advent of F1 the popularity of formula racing in India is treading an exponential curve, making the media take note of TeamKART more and more. We always try to highlight our relationship with the sponsors and express our gratitude via the same media. But its not only our public outreach that we bring to the table, sponsoring us also provides access to highly motivated and skilled potential employees. Our members make it possible to take the car from the drawing board to the race track. Being part of TeamKART not only helps members to gain tremendous technical and practical know-how of the field but also familiarize them with team work, organization and project management, giving them an edge over other graduates. Also association with a student led research project helps boost the image of any company. If you feel interested in the idea of partnering with us, please contact us.
