Chef DePie's stupid waiters have managed to misplace a large number of pies inside his restaurant.
The customers are waiting. Use WASD or arrows to move DePie.
Find as many pies as you can as they appear before the timer runs out.

Time Bonus: Every 3 pies served will convince your customers to wait another some more and will add 1 second to the clock.

Each pie gives a base score of 6 points.

Perfect Catch:Follow a path of shortest number of steps for a perfect catch.

Score Bonus:Combo perfect catches add bonus points to the scoreboard.

Score penalty: Imperfect catches gives a penalty of 1 point per extra step till point to be awarded becomes zero and also resets combo meter.

Pause: To pause game press Alt or switch to another tab or window.
