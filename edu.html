School and College stuff

Education is one of the most important aspects in my life. I believe it is the one thing that separates man and animal. Our education is what keeps us on our right track.

My education involved a lot of travelling and I always ended up studying in faraway places. Schooling was completed through 5 different schools over 3 districts in the state of Kerala, India. On the bright side I have connections with 5 batchs of students my age.
